Reserved exclusively for our full-service clients, WSE Event Agency's custom fabrication crafts a 3D brand moment that comes alive as a multi-sensory representation of your brand ethos.

WSE Event Agency takes your brand as seriously as you do, which means we never utilize standard or off-the-shelf setups. 

Without exception, Kate Goddard and her team custom design, build, and fabricate experiences that deliver bespoke immersion and impressive personalization that bring your brand to life. 

ALL THE BUZZ

Through strategic use of tailored builds in combination with textures, plants, wood tones, materials, lighting, and more, your guests are transported into the heart of your headquarters, fostering a sense of belonging and brand immersion. 

Whether you have a vision or would like our creative team to bring your brand to life, we exclusively build large-scale experiential events from the ground up.

  • Custom Fabricated Signage
    Reinforce brand recognition and make a bold statement with personalized signage that captures the essence of your brand.

  • Demo Booths
    Elevate engagement with your products or services with our branded demo booths.

  • Tailored to your brand, these sets are designed as a creative branding opportunity with the intention of sharing.

  • Custom Stage Design
    From dynamic backdrops to eye-catching centerpieces, ensure that your stage becomes a captivating focal point.

  • Whether it's branded furniture, interactive displays, or thematic experiential elements, let your imagination run wild. We'll turn your custom design concepts into tangible, memorable experiences that set your event apart.

  • Art Installations
    From visually striking sculptures to immersive artistic experiences, WSE creates brand-worthy impressions.

WSE Event Agency's custom fabrication creates moments worth sharing exclusively for our full-service clients.
